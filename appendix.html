Appendix

Declarations • Principles • Prayer

Prophetic Declarations

  1. I am not defined by labels. I am defined by His Word.
  2. My home is a dwelling place for His presence.
  3. The fire of revival burns brighter within me.
  4. I choose holiness over compromise.
  5. Chains of fear and shame are broken in Jesus' name.
  6. From one flame to a thousand fires.
  7. I am a carrier of revival.

Revival Principles

  1. Presence over performance.
  2. Truth and love together.
  3. Holiness remains non negotiable.
  4. Community fuels fire.
  5. Obedience sustains revival.
  6. Transformation multiplies revival.
  7. Guard the flame.

Prayer For Revival

Father, let Your fire fall again. Awaken homes, churches, and nations. Break fear, shame, and false identity. Raise up people who carry truth, holiness, and love. From one flame to a thousand fires, let Your glory fill the earth. In Jesus' name, amen.
