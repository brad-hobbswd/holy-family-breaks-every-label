## About The Story

*The Rise of a Holy Family: A Revival Born from Rejection* followed a journey through rejection, restoration, and healing. Voices from the past attempted to shape identity while Jesus began rebuilding what had been broken.

The story continues.

Revival still burns.

Labels still break.

A family shaped through hardship now faces larger questions surrounding identity, belonging, truth, culture, and faith. A fire once hidden inside a farmhouse begins spreading beyond walls, communities, and borders.

What begins with one family becomes a movement.

From one flame to a thousand fires.
