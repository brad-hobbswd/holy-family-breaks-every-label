Chapter 3

The Weight of Labels

The living room glowed with firelight, yet the atmosphere felt heavy. Headlines screamed from every screen. Words filled with accusation and assumption echoed louder than truth.

Daniel sat quietly. His hands trembled around his journal. Questions had entered their home. Questions about identity. Questions about belonging. Questions carrying pain.

Caleb looked at his son. Compassion filled his eyes. Truth and love stood together in the room. Neither would abandon the other.

Outside, the world kept speaking labels. Inside, another voice rose stronger. "You are His."
