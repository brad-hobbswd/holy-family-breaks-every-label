About Bradley Hobbs

Author • Revival Writer • Faith Journey Storyteller

The Story Behind The Writing

Bradley Hobbs writes stories centered around family, restoration, healing, identity, and revival. His work explores journeys through rejection, hope, and redemption while pointing readers toward Jesus.

The Rise of a Holy Family series follows families through pain, perseverance, and awakening. Themes of truth, grace, restoration, and revival weave through every page.
